Intro

Codewars is an educational community for computer programming. On the platform, software developers train on programming challenges known as kata. See more at codewars.com.

As a Codewars warrior, you are familiar with the different rewards earned through completing code challenges to move to other Katas (levels). When starting out, a warrior may have a difficult time understanding the points system and charting their progress. This app provides a snapshot of your progress and calculates what actions are necessary to reach the next level.

How to use

A Codewar username can be provided in the input section to see the warrior's information, including rank level, honor points, and other statistics.

The calculator function in the app helps to chart the number of Kyu (challenges) if you have a specific goal in mind.
